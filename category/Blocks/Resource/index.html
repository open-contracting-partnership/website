How we measure impact

Sustained implementation of open contracting takes time and rarely proceeds on a smooth, linear path. We set a high bar for measuring impact and don’t stop and simply publishing data. For us, impact means significant, widespread, documented change where we can show both a shift in behavior and the outcomes when we look at competition, savings, better services, and/or improvement in governance or public trust.

We work on the ground with partners across government, business, and civil society to catalyze and assist specific local reforms at the city, regional and national level.

We have developed resources that help you with measuring impact from the start of procurement reforms and we regularly write about what we have learned from our interventions.

We can help measure your impact.
